When is art good?

March 2, 2026 •

When it is a pure expression of the soul. When the artist steps aside. Without his stories, needs and wants. His art might come alive.

When art is born, it’s born alone. Do you believe you played your hand? When you write, you write your soul. Your play is blocking you the way.

And when you shine, you shine alone. With God, the stories melt. And untouched art your only crown. The you, you gave away.

The artist’s path to purge through art. A loyal to his soul. And one day, the treachery ends in authentic soul expression.
