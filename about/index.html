About Writing

Your art will take you home.

Interests make me zigzag through life. I consolidate my journey here.

I birth songs (V1; Current experiments) since age 15. I write quite a bit, and read quite a lot. I have published a few books (the God and Art book series and a Children's book) and hosted a podcast (V1 shut down). I am learning to code and am working towards my Computer Science degree (View projects). The why for the zig and the how for the zag I left behind. Can't it just be fun for me?

The most exciting part of my life is my fiancée, of course. Here are a few pictures of us (I'm the smile on the left). We are happy.

Be useful, yes. But satisfy yourself.

There's no subscribe button on purpose. I love my prose, but I write for myself. When I think I write for you, I do just that. For a long time, I lost myself. This space will remind me of the origin of my joy. A gift from myself to myself. When you reflect and collapse into your work, and your work is left. Isn't that why we do art? Stillness is where God resides, and our coming home marks the end of reaching. How can one go to a place one never left?
